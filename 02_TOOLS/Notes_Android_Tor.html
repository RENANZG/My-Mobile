Introduction

Orbot will not make your activity in other apps anonymous, but it will hide it from your network. If you want to browse anonymously, use Tor Browser.

Vantages and disvantages of routing connection throug tor in mobile

Aspect Vantages Disvantages
Security Encrypts internet traffic, hiding actual location and IP address Risk of leakage if not properly configured
Anonymity Provides anonymity by routing connection through thousands of computers worldwide Many apps and websites may consider you as a bot, limiting functionality
Privacy Protects against traffic analysis and network surveillance Some apps, like Reddit or Instagram, may not work due to Tor routing
Accessibility Allows access to onion services and hidden websites May encounter a lot of Captcha challenges, slowing down browsing
Performance Can be slower than direct connections due to multiple hops May experience slower speeds and higher latency
Usability Can be used to bypass censorship and access restricted content May require additional configuration and setup, potentially confusing for non-tech-savvy users

Setting Orbot in VPN Mode on Android

  1. In Orbot, select the apps that you want to route through Tor. Only these selected apps will be able to connect to the internet through Tor.
  2. In your Android's VPN settings, enable "Block connections without VPN". This will prevent any app that tries to connect outside the Tor network from accessing the internet.
  3. In your Wi-Fi settings, choose "Modify" and set the following:
    • Hostname: 127.0.0.1
    • Port: 8118
    Save your changes.
  4. Go to "More connections" (or "More" or "Advanced" depending on your phone model) and choose "VPN".
  5. In Orbot, go to the settings and enable the following:
    • "Always-on VPN": ON
    • "Only allow connections through VPN": ON
    This will help prevent most leaks and ensure that your internet connection is secure and private.
